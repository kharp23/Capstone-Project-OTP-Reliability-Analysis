# Capstone Project: OTP and Reliability Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes On-Time Performance (OTP) and Reliability of NJ Transit and Amtrak operations on the Northeast Corridor (NEC) between January 2020 and May 2020.

## Research Questions

The research is guided by three primary questions:
1)	What factors contribute to train delays in year 2020 (January through May) on NEC?
a.	Route or Station Reliability: Identify stations and routes experiencing highest frequency and duration of delays to focus on operational bottleneck and infrastructure inefficiencies.? Does certain route perform worse than others in terms of punctuality?
b.	Temporal trend, Time based Delay: How do train delay patterns change over time: Peak hour congestion Analysis, Day of the week analysis, Seasonal comparison. If rush hour significantly increase variability in performance.
2)	If measurable difference in delay behaviour between NJ Transit and Amtrak on same corridor (NEC)?
3)	By using the historical data, if ML model can be used to predict the future delays or performance?


## Dataset
Source: Kaggle
Period: January 2020 – May 2020
